Chi Siamo

Run Society is more than just a running club; it's a vibrant community built on friendship, shared experiences, and a love for running. We believe in creating a safe and inclusive space where runners of all levels can come together, connect, and enjoy the journey. Our philosophy centers around the joy of running, the camaraderie of group activities, and the exploration of our beautiful city, Trieste, and its surroundings.

What sets us apart is our commitment to fostering a welcoming environment for everyone. We are a no-fee club, meaning there are no membership costs to join our runs and events. We believe that running should be accessible to all, and we strive to remove any barriers that might prevent people from participating.

Beyond the runs, we organize a variety of social activities that make our club unique. From our relaxed 'Easy Aperitivo' gatherings after runs, to enjoying a refreshing 'Gelatino' together on warm evenings, and even taking a 'Bagnetto' (swim) in the sea after a coastal run, we believe in celebrating our shared passion for running and life. These social events are an integral part of our club culture, providing opportunities to connect, build friendships, and create lasting memories.

Whether you're a seasoned marathoner or just starting your running journey, Run Society welcomes you with open arms. Join us and experience the joy of running in a supportive and fun-loving community.
